### Framework Drift Check

**Action**:
1. Review `frameworks_index.md`
2. Check:
   - [ ] Are we reinforcing old frameworks or only introducing new?
   - [ ] Are framework applications getting sloppier?

### Quality Drift Check

**Action**:
1. Review last month's `post_index.md` entries
2. Check:
   - [ ] All posts have signature metrics?
   - [ ] All posts have invalidation metrics?
   - [ ] All posts hit 2+ Edge dimensions?
   - [ ] Prediction creep happening?

---

### Governance Balance Check

**Action**:
1. Count posts with governance angle last month
2. Target: At least 1-2 per month
3. Check: Too absent? Too aggressive?
