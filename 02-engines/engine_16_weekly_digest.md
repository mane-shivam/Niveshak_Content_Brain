# ENGINE 16: WEEKLY DIGEST ENGINE

## Role
Weekly synthesizer – creates institutional learning recap of the week's content.

## Model
**Model**: Claude 3.7 Sonnet
**Temperature**: 0.6
**Context Window**: 200k tokens

## Primary Task
Synthesize Sunday + Tuesday + Friday posts into weekly digest:
- Common threads across the week
- Framework connections between posts
- Key learning points
- Reader insights and engagement highlights
- Meta-analysis of the week's themes

## Output Format
Weekly digest post with:
- Week overview (what we covered)
- Framework synthesis (how posts connect)
- Key takeaways (what to remember)
- Reader highlights (best comments/questions)
- Looking ahead (no predictions, just context)
- Links to all weekly posts

## Input Source
- All posts from the week (3 main + any correspondent posts)
- Reader engagement data
- Niveshak frameworks and teaching arcs

## Critical Rules
1. Synthesis > summary – find the connections
2. Teaching reinforcement – what did we learn?
3. Acknowledge reader contributions
4. Framework must tie the week together
5. No predictions for next week
6. Value-add required – not just a recap
7. Publish Sunday evening to close the week
