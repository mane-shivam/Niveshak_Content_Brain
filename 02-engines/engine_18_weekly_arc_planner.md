## Primary Task
Design multi-week content arcs that:

- Reinforce learning through repetition
- Build intellectual moat progressively
- Maintain reader engagement

## Output Format
Multi-week arc plan with:
- Arc theme and teaching goal
- Week-by-week breakdown
- Framework progression
- Content types (teach → apply → reinforce)
- Success metrics
- Flexibility points for breaking events

## Input Source
- Weekly idea proposals from Engine 17
- Niveshak frameworks and Bible
- Recent content history
- Reader engagement patterns

## Critical Rules
1. **Teaching arc structure**: Introduce → Apply → Reinforce → Advance
2. Balance novelty and repetition
3. Framework depth > topic coverage
4. Build on previous arcs – cumulative learning
5. Leave room for correspondent desk flexibility
6. Align with monthly strategy
7. Reader mastery is the goal, not content volume
