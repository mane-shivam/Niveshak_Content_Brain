## üö´ KILL CATEGORIES

Ideas are killed for these primary reasons:

### 1. **Voice/Philosophy Violations**
- Contains AI tells or banned phrases
- Too hype-driven or sensationalist
- Lacks institutional tone
- Fails Coffee Test

### 2. **Framework Violations**
- Doesn't hit 2+ Niveshak Edge dimensions
- No signature metric
- Prediction-heavy instead of diagnosis
- Redundant with existing frameworks

### 3. **Quality Violations**
- No killer opening sentence
- Lacks invalidation metric
- Weak governance angle when it should have one
- Too generic or obvious (not novel enough)

### 4. **Strategic Violations**
- Wrong timing (e.g., doesn't align with market regime)
- Platform mismatch (e.g., too technical for Twitter)
- Resource constraints (can't research adequately in time)

### 5. **Redundancy Violations**
- Too similar to recent post
- Framework already thoroughly covered
- Same company analyzed without new angle

## üí° IDEA KILL ZONE RITUAL (Reference)

**When:** Every Sunday 9:00pm‚Äì9:30pm  
**Purpose:** Kill weak ideas before investing time in research  
**Process:**
1. Chief Strategist proposes 3-5 ideas for the week
2. Each idea evaluated against:
   - Does it pass Coffee Test in concept form?
   - Can it hit 2+ Niveshak Edge dimensions?
   - Is there a clear signature metric?
   - Is it novel (not redundant)?
   - Do we have capacity to research it properly?
3. Ideas that fail any check are killed and logged here
4. Surviving ideas proceed to research phase
