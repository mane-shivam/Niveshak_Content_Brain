## STRUCTURE

### Length
- **Target**: 800-1200 words
- **Format**: Multiple short paragraphs (2-3 lines each)
- **Whitespace**: Critical for readability

### Flow
1. **Hook paragraph**: Signature metric or insight
2. **Context**: Setup the framework
3. **Analysis**: Deep dive (can be longer than Twitter)
4. **Implications**: What this means for investors
5. **Invalidation**: What would prove this wrong
6. **CTA**: Thoughtful question to drive discussion

---
